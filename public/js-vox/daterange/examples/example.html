Basic DatePicker

DatePicker in the most basic form.
Code:
$('#datepicker').DatePicker({
  mode:"single",
  date: new Date(),
  inline: true
});

Range DatePicker

Allows picking between two dates. Click a date to mark an endpoint, then another to set other end point.
Code:
$('#datepicker').DatePicker({
  mode:"range",
  date: ['2012-10-20', new Date()],
  inline: true,
  calendars: 2
});

Two Ranges DatePicker

Demonstrates the new functionality of two date ranges.
Code:
$('#datepicker').DatePicker({
  mode:"tworanges",
  date: ['2012-10-20', '2012-10-29',
         '2012-10-10', '2012-10-19'],
  inline: true,
  calendars: 2
});
